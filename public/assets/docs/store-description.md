# App Store & Google Play Description

## Short Description (Promotional Text)
Organize, join, and manage tennis tournaments with ease. The ultimate platform for players and organizers!

## Full Description

**Entre Sets** is the ultimate tennis tournament management app, built for both passionate players and dedicated organizers. Whether you're running a local club tournament, a weekend round-robin, or simply looking to find your next competitive match, Entre Sets makes it seamless and professional.

### 🎾 For Players:
*   **Find Your Next Match:** Browse and register for tournaments in your area.
*   **Live Brackets & Schedules:** View real-time bracket updates, match times, and courts. Never miss a game with your personalized match calendar!
*   **Track Your Progress:** Keep your player profile updated, cheer for friends, and follow live match results.
*   **Instant Notifications:** Get instant alerts for schedule changes, match confirmations, and tournament announcements.
*   **Guided Onboarding:** Our interactive tutorial gets you familiar with the app in seconds.

### 🏆 For Organizers:
*   **Easy Tournament Creation:** Set up singles, doubles, mixed, and "Open Tennis" (Group Stage & Playoffs) formats in minutes.
*   **Custom Match Rules Builder:** Fine-tune the scoring (Sets to win, Advantage, Tiebreaks), set time limits, and configure walkover conditions.
*   **Smart Scheduling Engine:** Generate matchups automatically, handle player availabilities, and optimize your court time efficiently.
*   **Participant Management:** Accept or deny registrations, manage open slots, and keep track of who's playing.
*   **Share with Ease:** Generate deep links to share your tournament instantly via WhatsApp, social media, or email.

Built by players, for players. Entre Sets removes the headache of spreadsheets and confusing group chats, bringing professional-grade tournament management right to your phone. 

**Download Entre Sets today and elevate your game!**
